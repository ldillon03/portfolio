About

Hi! I'm Lauren, an aspiring multimedia content creator. I grew up in Greenville, South Carolina with two older sisters and lots of animals. I moved to Plano, Texas in 2020 where I attended John Paul II and participated on the Academic Decathlon team. After high school, I attended Tulane University's honors program, however I transferred to Baylor University in 2023 due to my type one diabetes diagnosis. The first few years of college learning to manage this disease 24/7 were extremely challenging, and I am still far from perfect, but it has led me to meet so many wonderful people in the community and learn so much about myself and my strength. I still wish for a cure, but I am so grateful for the person it has led me to become. My hobbies include reading, drawing, and being outside whenever I can. I'm passionate about writing, film, and storytelling, which is why I chose Communication Specialist as my major. While I was able to focus primarily on film & digital media courses, I appreciated that the program also provided more practical communication and rhetoric courses. I graduate in May, and I'm so excited to see where God leads me.
